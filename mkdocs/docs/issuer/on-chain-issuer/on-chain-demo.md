Aside from the [On-chain issuer tutorial](./on-chain-tutorial.md), we also provide a quick demonstration of how the on-chain issuer works in a demo version.
Please, follow the instructions on this page to issue a simple credential on chain.

1. Download Polygon ID mobile app
    - For Android: <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.polygonid.wallet" target="_blank">Polygon ID on Google Play</a>
    - For iOS: <a href="https://apps.apple.com/us/app/polygon-id/id1629870183" target="_blank">Polygon ID on the App Store</a>
2. Head to [the on-chain issuer demo](https://issuer-v2.polygonid.me/) and click on Sign Up. 
3. Authenticate with the app by scanning the QR code with the Polygon ID wallet app. Follow instructions on the app interface.
    
    <div align="center">
        <img src="../../../imgs/on-chain-issuer/demo/scan.png" width="300"></img>
    </div>

4. The on-chain issuer demo main page presents the claims previously issued. Click on **Create Claims**.
    
    <div align="center">
        <img src="../../../imgs/on-chain-issuer/demo/create.png" width="600"></img>
    </div>

5. Let us issue a simple age credential, attesting to the fact that the ID holder was born in a specific year as seen below.

    <div align="center">
        <img src="../../../imgs/on-chain-issuer/demo/claim.png" width="500"></img>
    </div>

    Click on the dropdown menu next to the **Submit** button and choose **Submit on-chain**.

6. Fetch the credential by scanning the QR code with your Polygon Id wallet app. Follow the instructions on the app.
    
    <div align="center">
        <img src="../../../imgs/on-chain-issuer/demo/credential.png" width="300"></img>
    </div>

7. You will be able to see the newly created credential on the app:

    <div align="center">
        <img src="../../../imgs/on-chain-issuer/demo/mobile.jpeg" width="300"></img>
    </div>

    And a reference to it on the on-chain issuer demo main page. 

    <div align="center">
        <img src="../../../imgs/on-chain-issuer/demo/credential-web.png" width="800"></img>
    </div>
